# SePRO Corporation Acquires Earth Science Laboratories, Strengthening Its Water Treatment Portfolio Across Drinking Water, Conveyance, and Invasive Species Management

> Note: provisional URL was resolved to a primary source. Original: https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260401609078/en/SePRO-Corporation-Acquires-Earth-Science-Laboratories-Strengthening-Its-Water-Treatment-Portfolio-Across-Drinking-Water-Conveyance-and-Invasive-Species-Management

**What it is.** A corporate press release (originally distributed via Business Wire on/around April 1, 2026, and syndicated by Wastewater Digest, an AP wire pickup carried on AM 970 The Answer, and Pulse2.com) announcing that SePRO Corporation acquired — and per a related law-firm notice, "merged with" — Earth Science Laboratories, Inc. (ESL), a Rogers, Arkansas-based water-treatment company, adding ESL's EarthTec and EarthTec QZ algaecide / invasive-mussel-control products to SePRO's portfolio; no financial terms were disclosed.
