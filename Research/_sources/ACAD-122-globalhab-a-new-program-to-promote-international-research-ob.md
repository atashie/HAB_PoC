# GlobalHAB: A New Program to Promote International Research, Observations, and Modeling of Harmful Algal Blooms in Aquatic Systems

**What it is.** A short (12-page) 2017 Oceanography journal program-overview/perspective article by Berdalet, Kudela, Urban and 13 co-authors that announces GlobalHAB, a new IOC(UNESCO)/SCOR-cosponsored international program for harmful algal bloom (HAB) research, observations, and modeling — presented as the successor to the 1998–2013 GEOHAB program, explicitly extended to cover freshwater systems and the effects of HABs on human society in a changing world.
