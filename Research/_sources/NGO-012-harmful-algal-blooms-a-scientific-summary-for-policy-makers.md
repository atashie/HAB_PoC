# Harmful Algal Blooms: A Scientific Summary for Policy Makers

> Note: provisional URL was resolved to a primary source. Original: https://unesdoc.unesco.org/ark:/48223/pf0000233419

**What it is.** A 2015 policy-facing scientific synthesis on harmful algal blooms (HABs), published by UNESCO's Intergovernmental Oceanographic Commission (report IOC/INF-1320) and prepared by the SCOR-IOC Scientific Steering Committee of the GEOHAB programme (Global Ecology and Oceanography of Harmful Algal Blooms, 1998-2014). Per its catalog abstract, it explains what HABs are and surveys their causes, ecosystem/societal impacts, and mitigation/management options for a policy audience; the report's own body text could not be retrieved (see fetch_notes), so this dossier entry is built only from its catalog abstract and bibliographic metadata.
