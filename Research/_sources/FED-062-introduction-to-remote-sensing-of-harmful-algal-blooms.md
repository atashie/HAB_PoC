# Introduction to Remote Sensing of Harmful Algal Blooms

**What it is.** A four-part (one hour per session), introductory, instructor-led NASA ARSET (Applied Remote Sensing Training Program) webinar course delivered September 5–26, 2017, that teaches practitioners how satellite remote sensing — ocean-color sensors plus coupled model/remote-sensing decision-support systems — can be used to detect and monitor both marine and freshwater harmful algal blooms. It is a training curriculum aimed at management/practitioner organizations, not an original research paper, and it carries its own disclaimer that the methods taught may since have been superseded.
